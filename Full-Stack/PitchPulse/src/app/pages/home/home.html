Under the Hood

From microphone to music notation

1

Audio Capture

The Web Audio API streams raw PCM data from your microphone in real time.

2

FFT Analysis

A Fast Fourier Transform resolves the signal into frequency bins, isolating the fundamental pitch.

3

ML Classification

A trained model maps frequencies to musical notes and filters background noise.

4

Live Score

Notes appear on a virtual staff and are saved to your session history.
